Playing Nots & Crosses

This is the computer version of the popular game Nots and Crosses which is also called as Tic Tac Toe. 
Rules: A player choices any column choosing the corresponding digit. His symbol (X/O) appears in the column.
The next player does the same. This will be repeated till all the column are occupied. To win: A Player must capture three corresponding column such as 1,2,3 or 4,5,6 or 7,8,9 or 1,5,9 or 3,5,7 or 1,4,7 or 2,5,8 or 3,6,9.
